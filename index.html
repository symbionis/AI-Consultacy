My role isn't to teach you AI. It's to understand you, in the way you actually work. Your habits, your rhythms, the shape of your day. And then to weave the tools in, at your pace.

When you run a business or a small team, your day is already full. What I offer isn't AI. It's a way of bringing it into your work that augments how you already operate, without disrupting it.

About Frank

Frank Sykes coaches entrepreneurs and small teams through the AI transition. He's been here before. In 1998, during the internet revolution, he built one of London's first online job-matching systems and watched the consultants who used it move from "this is going to take our job away" to, three months later, the opposite.

Twenty years in London, then Geneva for his son. The technology has changed. The shape of what happens when it arrives hasn't.

What this is, in three parts.

01 / Understanding

A humanistic diagnostic.

I offer you a real conversation about your univers, what you're carrying, what's been waiting, and where you want to make more room. The whole activity, the whole person. Not the tools, but the things that keep coming back and weighing on you.

You leave with a clear read of where you stand. And two experiments to try this week.

02 / Continuity

Six sessions, alongside you.

A journey of six weekly sessions, alongside you. We get to know how you actually operate, what you're carrying, and where to start. Then we shape it together and refine it until the weight lifts.

A project you've been carrying moves forward. Your mental load lifts.

03 / A system you keep

Yours, after we stop working together.

I leave you with a working system shaped to your operation, one you understand and can evolve. Your own workflows, your own routines, custom skills that work alongside you.

If our engagement ends, your proposals still draft themselves. Your Monday brief still arrives.
